Chapter 01

The Foundation

Why "hiring" an AI is fundamentally different from using one.

The Mindset Shift

Most people use AI like a search engine with extra steps. They ask questions, get answers, copy-paste results. That's not hiring — that's consulting.

Hiring means giving the AI a role, responsibilities, tools, and autonomy. It means the AI has a job, not a task.

The difference: a task-worker waits for instructions. An employee anticipates needs and acts independently within their domain.

Platform Options

OpenClaw Full-featured, multi-channel, persistent memory. Best for production agents.
Claude Projects Good for focused work. Limited tool access. Best for analysis tasks.
Custom Stack Maximum flexibility. Requires engineering. Best for specific workflows.
